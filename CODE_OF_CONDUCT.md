# Code of Conduct

Local Lead Finder aims to be a practical, respectful open-source project.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive;
- assume good intent while still being clear about problems;
- avoid harassment, insults, personal attacks, and discriminatory language;
- respect privacy and do not post sensitive lead, customer, or personal data;
- keep discussions focused on responsible open-data workflows.

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to ricardosuper11@gmail.com.
